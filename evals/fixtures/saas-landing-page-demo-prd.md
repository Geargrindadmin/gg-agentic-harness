# SaaS Landing Page Demo PRD

## Product

Build a launch-ready marketing site for a B2B SaaS company called `SignalForge`.

SignalForge helps operations and revenue teams automate repetitive back-office work across CRM, billing, support, and internal approvals.

## Audience

- VP of Operations
- Revenue Operations leaders
- Mid-market COO / Head of Systems
- IT and automation buyers evaluating enterprise workflow platforms

## Primary Goal

Generate qualified demo requests from enterprise buyers who want process automation without building fragile internal tooling.

## Core Narrative

SignalForge turns messy, manual operating work into reliable, governed automations. The product should feel enterprise-ready, credible, and practical rather than futuristic or gimmicky.

## Required Site Outcomes

- strong homepage with clear positioning
- compelling hero section
- proof and trust section
- feature overview section
- use-case section
- pricing section
- case studies section
- FAQ section
- final CTA/contact funnel
- top navigation and footer

## Brand Direction

- tone: credible, modern, enterprise, confident
- visual style: refined SaaS brand, premium but restrained
- avoid: crypto aesthetics, neon cyberpunk, generic startup fluff, exaggerated AI jargon

## Messaging Requirements

- emphasize reliability, governance, and measurable operational leverage
- explain the value in plain language
- include metrics-oriented proof points
- include one primary CTA: `Book a Demo`
- include one secondary CTA: `See Platform Overview`

## Product Themes To Highlight

- workflow orchestration across business systems
- approvals and exception handling
- observability and auditability
- role-aware automation and policy controls
- faster launch time than internal tooling

## Required Content Blocks

### Hero

- headline focused on automating complex operations
- supporting copy with business outcome language
- primary and secondary CTAs

### Proof

- customer or outcome metrics
- recognizable trust logos or logo placeholders
- short credibility copy

### Features

- orchestration
- governance
- observability
- integrations

### Use Cases

- quote-to-cash workflows
- support escalation automation
- finance approvals
- renewal and onboarding operations

### Pricing

- tiered pricing table suitable for SaaS buyers
- pricing is informational only for this demo
- do not generate checkout, subscriptions, billing APIs, or payment provider integrations
- enterprise CTA path

### Case Studies

- at least three compact case study cards with measurable outcomes

### FAQ

- implementation speed
- security/governance
- integrations
- team ownership

### Final CTA

- concise contact/demo capture section
- clear executive-level value proposition

## Technical Expectations

- output should resolve to the `marketing-site` lane
- include design-system and observability oriented structure where supported
- generate a Next.js bundle that can install and run locally
- keep content editable and documentation-friendly
- no live billing or payment integration should be included in this demo build

## Success Criteria

- page looks credible enough for an enterprise SaaS launch mock
- structure supports conversion and storytelling
- build installs cleanly
- local preview runs without manual code cleanup
